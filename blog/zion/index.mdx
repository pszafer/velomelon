Every time I think of Zion I say Ohhhh. It wasn't just beautiful, the ride through it was so exciting and thrilling that I ate a lot of bugs with my dropped mouth ;) 
<youtube>7BgAIXJqYJs</youtube>
We got there on Mount Carmel Hwy but we had to hitch a ride through the tunnel. After that there was a long "feel so alive" ride downhill. Sculptured rocks were like huge masterpieces made by artists of natural powers. We hiked Angels Landing Trail which was challenging. No wonder seven people died there since 2004. You can climb there using chains because it's pretty steep and not much space to keep your balance. But we did it :) 
Wonderful time!!!
