_"Everybody needs beauty as well as bread, places to play in and pray in, where nature may heal and give strength to body and soul."_ - John Muir. I agree with that man. In March We spent two days in Yosemite and we gained faith and power again to carry on our trip.

<youtube>Bskct5Zqkxc</youtube>

In two days we visited Upper Yosemite Fall, Lower Yosemite Fall and Nevada Fall.
In this time of the year they look amazing!!!
