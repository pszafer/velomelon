After leaving the South Rim of Grand Canyon we rode down to totally different world. Green pine forest was gone and it was getting more and more colorful and warm. Spring woke up all the beauty in this part of Arizona. We were amazed by spectacular plants that grow there. Warm rocks and sandy ground were ornamented by pink flowers of Prickly Pear Cacti, creamy petals of Banana Yucca, yellowish Rubber Rabbtitbrush and many, many more.

<youtube>R9ZKz9FUh1o</youtube>
We took route 64, then hwy 89 and in Gap we turned to Copper Mine Road going to Page.
Amazing tour!
